About JB Hospital

Your trusted partner in healthcare excellence

At JB Hospital, we believe that healthcare is more than just treating illnesses — it’s about caring for people with compassion, dignity, and respect. Established with a vision to provide world-class medical care close to home, JB Hospital brings together expert doctors, advanced technology, and a patient-first approach.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional medical care with compassion and innovation, making high-quality healthcare accessible to all.

History & legacy

Founded in 2005, JB Hospital has grown from a modest healthcare facility into a multi-specialty hospital serving thousands of patients each year. Our journey began with a simple mission — to make high-quality healthcare accessible to everyone, regardless of background or circumstance. Over the years, our commitment to patient care, clinical excellence, and ethical practice has earned us the trust of the community.

Our Facilities

We are equipped with:

  • State-of-the-art Operation Theatres with advanced surgical technology.
  • A 24/7 Emergency Department with rapid response teams.
  • Modern Diagnostic Labs and imaging facilities including MRI, CT, and Ultrasound.
  • Comfortable Patient Wards and private rooms designed for healing.
  • A fully functional Pharmacy available round the clock.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Treating each patient like family.

  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and medical advancements.

Community Engagement

Beyond medical care, JB Hospital actively participates in health camps, blood donation drives, and free check-up programs to promote wellness in the community. We believe that healthcare begins with awareness, and we work tirelessly to spread medical education and preventive care practices.
